About Our Institution

Empowering Students to Achieve Their True Potential

Located prominently on NH-68 in Baldev Nagar, Barmer, Daisy Days International School operates under the esteemed guidance of the Jahanvi Sansthan. We are dedicated to creating a vibrant, inspiring, and safe learning environment for children from their early years through senior secondary education.

As a CBSE-affiliated institution, we integrate traditional pedagogical approaches with modern technology. Our "House" system instills teamwork, while our state-of-the-art facilities ensure students excel not just academically, but in sports, arts, and leadership.

  • Safe Environment: Secure campus located opposite Canara Bank.
  • Expert Faculty: Highly trained and dedicated teaching staff.
  • Co-Curriculars: Drama, dance, debates, and extensive sports facilities.
