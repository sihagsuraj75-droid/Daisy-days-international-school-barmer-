Situated prominently on NH-68 in Baldev Nagar, Barmer, Daisy Days International School has been a beacon of learning since 2015. We are dedicated to providing a vibrant, secure, and technologically advanced environment for children.

Our renowned "House" program focuses on holistic development—instilling teamwork, self-reliance, and leadership through academics, sports, and cultural activities like drama and debate.

CBSE Curriculum

We offer a structured, progressive learning environment divided into key stages to maximize student potential.

Early Years

Nursery & Playgroup focus on sensory-motor skills, play-way methods, and foundational literacy in a safe, colorful setting.

  • Activity-based learning
  • Child-centric approach

Middle School

Classes 1 to 8 transition into structured subjects, inquiry-based learning, Maths labs, and language proficiency.

  • English & Hindi Language Labs
  • Co-curricular integration

Senior Secondary

Classes 9 to 12 prepare students for board exams with specialized streams (PCM, PCB, Commerce, Arts) and career guidance.

  • Advanced Science Labs
  • Board exam preparation
